### The Ermine bike offers high speed and does not require pedaling.
Unlike all other E-Bikes on the D/A/CH market, this one is an E-Bike, and not a Pedelec.
##### Motor toggle button
##### Max. speed 35 km/h
##### Max. range: ca. 80 km
##### 5 Assist modes
##### Motor: 500W
##### Battery: 48V 480 Wh
##### Charge time: 4-6h
##### 21 gears
##### 27,5" tires
